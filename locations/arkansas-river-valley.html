The Arkansas River Valley experiences extreme summer temperatures and high humidity. This intense heat causes asphalt shingles to expand, dry out, and become brittle. If the attic is poorly ventilated, this thermal shock can cause shingles to curl, cup, and lose their protective granules prematurely. We prevent thermal failure by installing balanced attic ventilation systems that maintain airflow and lower deck temperatures.
