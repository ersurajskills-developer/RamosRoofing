Hail damage is often invisible from the ground and rarely leaks immediately. Hail strikes bruise the asphalt matting of shingles, causing the protective mineral granules to loosen and wash away. Over the next 12 to 24 months, the exposed asphalt degrades under UV rays, leading to brittleness, cracking, and leaks. A professional inspection is the only way to detect these strikes early enough to file a timely insurance claim.
