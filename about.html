A 25-Year Second Generation Story

Ramos Roofing was founded at the turn of the millennium with a single crew and a simple vision: to provide homeowners in the Arkansas River Valley with roofing services built on absolute integrity.

Today, the business is owned and operated by Austin Ramos alongside his father. While we have grown from a small family operation into a leading regional contractor with 6+ specialized crews, we have never lost touch with the hands-on values that earned us our strong reputation.

We manage both residential roof replacements and large commercial TPO systems. Our business has grown organically through word-of-mouth recommendations, door-to-door relationships, and standing ready to help our communities rebuild after major storm and hail events.

Rapid Dispatch & Support Area

Our 6+ crews are dispatched from our Fort Smith headquarters, covering all of Northwest Arkansas (Bentonville, Fayetteville, Rogers), the Arkansas River Valley, and parts of Oklahoma.

Hassle-Free Restoration

We Work With Insurance

Navigating storm damage claims can be overwhelming. At Ramos Roofing Plus, we work directly with all major insurance carriers (such as State Farm, Allstate, Liberty Mutual, and others) to ensure your property restoration is handled smoothly.

Our specialists provide comprehensive damage inspections, detailed photographic evidence, and itemized estimates to support your claim from start to finish. Do not worry about the paperwork—we guide you through every step.

Storm Damage Claim Process

  • 1
    Free On-Site Assessment We inspect the shingles, deck, gutters, and siding to document all wind or hail impacts.
  • 2
    Submit Claim with Evidence Use our digital photo logs and structural reports as supporting documentation for your adjuster.
  • 3
    Adjuster Meeting Support If requested, we can meet the adjuster on-site to ensure all damaged sections are fully documented.
