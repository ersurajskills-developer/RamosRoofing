Clear and Transparent Warranty Coverage

A warranty is only as good as the company that stands behind it. Ramos Roofing Plus provides transparent, transferable warranties that offer real protection without hidden loopholes.

Our workmanship warranty covers our labor for 5 to 10 years after installation. This is separate from, and works alongside, the manufacturer's limited lifetime warranty on materials (like TAMKO's or Malarkey's product warranties).

We design our warranties to be clear and straightforward, ensuring you have peace of mind without overlapping pre-existing material coverages.

Frequently Asked Questions

A manufacturer's warranty covers defects in the roofing materials (such as shingles cracking or losing granules prematurely). A workmanship warranty is provided by the contractor and covers installation errors (such as leaks caused by improper flashing or nail placement). Both warranties are essential for complete protection.

We are certified installers for leading manufacturers, including TAMKO and Malarkey. These certifications are only awarded to contractors who meet strict training, insurance, and quality standards. Our certified status allows us to offer extended manufacturer systems warranties that cover both materials and labor.

Standard architectural shingles carry a limited lifetime manufacturer's material warranty. This typically includes a non-prorated period (often 10 to 15 years) where the manufacturer covers replacement costs. After this period, the warranty value depreciates based on the age of the roof.

An extended systems warranty is a premium warranty backed by the manufacturer that covers both material defects and contractor installation workmanship for up to 50 years. To qualify, your roof must be installed by a certified contractor like Ramos Roofing Plus using a complete set of the manufacturer's matching components.

Yes, most premium manufacturer warranties are transferable to a new owner, typically within a specific window (often 30 days) following the sale of the home. Transferring your roof warranty adds value to your property and provides peace of mind to the buyer. Contact our office to assist with the transfer paperwork.

A shingle warranty can be voided by improper installation (such as using too few nails or placing them outside the nail line), poor attic ventilation that causes shingles to bake from underneath, installing shingles over an existing shingle layer (overlay), or structural movement of the roof deck.

Yes, we back all our roof repair services with a dedicated workmanship warranty. The length of the repair warranty depends on the scope and condition of the surrounding roof structure. We provide clear, written warranty details in all our repair estimates.

Ramos Roofing Plus manages the entire warranty registration process for you. Once your roof replacement is completed and the final payment is processed, we submit the required documentation and purchase the warranty certificate from the manufacturer, sending you the official paperwork.

We offer manufacturer material warranties (typically 20 years on TPO membranes) and our workmanship warranty. For commercial projects, we also offer NDL (No Dollar Limit) manufacturer warranties, which cover the complete cost of leak repairs (materials and labor) for up to 20 or 30 years.

If you suspect a material defect, contact Ramos Roofing Plus first. We will perform an inspection, document the issue with photos, and help you submit the claim to the manufacturer. We guide you through the process to ensure a smooth resolution.
