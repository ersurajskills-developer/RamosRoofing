Storm Recovery

Restoring Safety and Curb Appeal

The weather in Arkansas and Oklahoma can be incredibly volatile. High straight-line winds can tear shingles clean off their nails, while heavy debris can compromise your roof's flashing and structural decking.

Ramos Roofing Plus specializes in full storm damage recovery. We perform thorough physical examinations to identify wind-induced shingle crease lines, torn flashing, and hidden structural damage that typical contractors ignore.

We deploy our local, factory-trained crews to stabilize and repair wind losses, replacing compromised shingle modules or metal panels with brand new materials to ensure that your home's envelope remains secure.

Restoration Services

  • Physical Storm Loss Assessment (Documenting shingle lift and structure shifts)
  • Complete Material Restoration (Replacing compromised shingles and metal panels)
  • Itemized Photo Reports (Detailed storm documentation for claims)

Frequently Asked Questions

Storm damage roof repair involves addressing the damage caused by severe weather. This includes replacing blown-off or creased shingles, repairing damaged valley liners and metal flashings, replacing hail-damaged roof vents, and repairing structural decking from fallen tree branches.

Yes, sudden damage caused by hail, wind, thunderstorms, and tornadoes is typically covered by standard homeowner's policies. We provide detailed photo documentation and itemized estimates to support your claim and help you navigate the process.

We prioritize storm damage repairs. If a storm has caused immediate leaks or structural damage, call us. We will deploy a crew to tarp the damaged areas and protect your home's interior, and then schedule permanent repairs using matching materials.

If the damage is limited to a specific section, we can replace the damaged shingles and flashings. However, if the storm has caused widespread hail bruising or broken sealant bonds across multiple facets, a complete replacement is required to ensure long-term reliability.

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency tarping and patching. If a storm strikes and causes an active leak, call Brandon at (479) 652-1424. We will dispatch a crew to install temporary waterproof tarps to protect your ceilings and flooring until permanent repairs are made.

If you file an insurance claim, your out-of-pocket cost is typically limited to your policy deductible. For non-insurance repairs, the cost depends on the size of the damaged area and the materials required. We provide clear, itemized written estimates.

We source matching shingle profiles and colors from top manufacturers. If your roof is older and shingles have faded from sun exposure, we match the faded code as closely as possible to ensure the patch blends cleanly with your existing roof.

Leaks can be caused by wind-driven rain getting under shingles where the sealant bond was broken, or water seeping through hair-cracks in hail-bruised asphalt matting. Flashings around chimneys and vent pipes can also be loosened by wind gusts.

Yes, all our repair work is backed by our workmanship warranty. If a complete replacement is required, you also receive manufacturer material warranties. As certified contractors, we can offer extended systems warranties covering both materials and labor.
