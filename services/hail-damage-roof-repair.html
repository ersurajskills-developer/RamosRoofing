Hail Damage Roof Repair

Expert hail damage roof repair in NWA and Fort Smith. We replace fractured shingles, dented flashing, and restore impact-compromised roofing systems.

Impact Mitigation

Fixing the Fractures that Threaten Your Deck

Hailstones strike roofs at terminal velocity, causing "bruises" that fracture the underlying asphalt mats of shingles. Over time, these hairline fractures allow water to seep through the shingle, rotting the deck timber beneath.

At Ramos Roofing Plus, we specialize in detecting and documenting hail impact damage. We mark and document hail strikes on shingles, metal panels, and soft metal vents (like turtle vents and box gutters) so you have clear evidence of the storm loss.

We replace hail-damaged components with brand new, weather-tested materials. We also offer upgrades to Class 4 impact-resistant shingles (like Malarkey Bella Vista) to protect your home against future hail events.

Frequently Asked Questions

General roof repair addresses wear and tear, like dried pipe boots or loose shingles. Hail damage roof repair specifically addresses the fractures and granule loss caused by hail strikes. It requires identifying bruised areas, replacing damaged soft metals, and compiling the detailed documentation needed for insurance claims.

Signs of hail damage include circular black spots on shingles (exposed asphalt), an unusual amount of shingle granules in your gutters or downspouts, and dents in soft metals like roof vents, valleys, and gutters. A professional roof inspection is the only way to verify damage.

In most states, insurance companies cannot raise your individual rates for filing a claim due to natural 'Acts of God' like hail storms. However, rates can rise regionally after major storm events. Filing a claim ensures you get the funds needed to repair your roof before leaks occur.

Bruised shingles have lost their protective granules and must be replaced to prevent asphalt degradation and leaks. We remove the damaged shingles, inspect the underlayment, and nail down matching shingles. If the bruising is widespread, a complete replacement is required.

Yes, we coordinate closely with insurance companies. We provide itemized estimates and photos of the hail damage. We also meet the insurance adjuster on-site at your property to walk the roof together, ensuring all damage is noted and agreed upon.

If you file an insurance claim, your out-of-pocket cost is typically limited to your policy deductible. For non-insurance repairs, the cost depends on the number of damaged shingles and flashings that need replacement. We provide clear, itemized estimates before starting work.

Large hail can crack shingles or damage flashings, causing immediate leaks. However, smaller hail typically causes granule loss that degrades shingles over 12 to 24 months before leaks appear. Inspecting your roof early allows you to address damage before leaks start.

We wrap your landscaping, gardens, and decks in heavy-duty protective tarps before beginning repairs. We also build wooden shields over AC units and windows. After completing the work, we perform a thorough cleanup and run magnets to capture all stray nails.

Standing seam metal roofs and Class 4 impact-resistant shingles are the most hail-resistant options. Class 4 shingles use rubberized polymers to absorb hail impacts without cracking. Both options protect your home and often qualify you for insurance discounts.
