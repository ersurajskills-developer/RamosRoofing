Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize in high-performance commercial flat and low-slope roofing systems. This includes single-ply TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) white membranes, multi-ply modified bitumen asphalt systems, standing seam commercial metal roofing, and elastomeric silicone coatings. We help you choose the best system based on your building's slope, usage, and budget.

TPO is highly popular for commercial buildings. Its heat-welded seams form a continuous, seamless waterproof barrier that prevents leaks. The highly reflective white surface bounces solar radiation away from your building. This reduces the heat load, resulting in significantly lower cooling costs and extended HVAC system life.

The lifespan of a commercial roof depends on the material. A professionally installed TPO or modified bitumen roof lasts 20 to 30 years with regular maintenance. Commercial metal roofs can last 50+ years. Applying an elastomeric coating can extend the life of an existing flat roof by 10 to 15 years at a fraction of tear-off costs.

Yes, commercial flat roof leaks can be difficult to locate because water travels along roof decks. Our commercial team is trained in advanced leak tracing. We inspect seam welds, checking expansion joints, flashing around HVAC curbs, and roof drains. We perform clean, heat-welded patches to restore waterproof integrity.

Major commercial roofing manufacturers require regular, professional maintenance to keep their system warranties valid. Accumulating debris, clogged drains causing ponding water, or unaddressed punctures can void your warranty. Our maintenance program provides the documentation and inspections required to keep your warranty secure.

Yes, if your commercial roof is structurally sound but leaking, we can apply an elastomeric acrylic or silicone coating. This liquid-applied coating seals all seams, penetrations, and micro-cracks, creating a seamless waterproof membrane. This restoration is highly cost-effective, avoids disposal fees, and is tax-deductible.

Yes, Ramos Roofing Plus is fully licensed (AR License # 039289, OK License # 800052) and carries comprehensive general liability and full workers' compensation insurance. Our crews are trained and certified by top commercial manufacturers to install, repair, and maintain their flat roofing systems.

Ponding water occurs when sections of a flat roof sag or when roof drains and scuppers are clogged. Standing water degrades roofing membranes and increases structural loads. We fix this by clearing drains, installing tapered insulation boards to create a positive slope toward the drains, or applying silicone coatings that resist standing water.
