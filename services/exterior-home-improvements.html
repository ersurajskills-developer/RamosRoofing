Complete Exteriors

The Details That Complete Your Home

A beautiful roof and quality siding need to be supported by solid trim, proper soffit ventilation, and an effective gutter system to function correctly.

Ramos Roofing Plus provides comprehensive exterior improvements. We install custom seamless aluminum gutters on-site, sized to capture rainfall and channel it safely away from your home's foundation.

We also inspect and repair soffit ventilation panels and wood fascia boards, preventing moisture buildup in your attic and sealing off entry points for squirrels, bats, and pests.

Frequently Asked Questions

We provide comprehensive exterior services, including new siding installations, complete siding replacements, gutter and gutter guard installations, soffit and fascia repairs, trim work, window wrap installations, and general storm damage restoration for residential properties.

We install insulated siding systems that feature a contoured foam backing, creating a continuous thermal barrier. We wrap windows and doors with flashing tape. This system blocks thermal bridging, reduces drafts, lowers energy bills, and improves indoor comfort.

For maximum durability, we recommend James Hardie fiber cement siding. It resists fire, pests, rot, and impact from hail, offering a 50+ year operational lifespan. We also install LP SmartSide engineered wood and low-maintenance insulated vinyl siding systems.

Yes, we custom-miter seamless aluminum gutters on-site at your property for a leak-free fit. We also install gutter guards (leaf screens) to prevent leaves and pine needles from clogging your gutters, which is essential to protect your foundation and eaves.

We coordinate siding, gutters, trim, and roofing installations together. This consolidated staging ensures that different phases of the project seal transition zones correctly, reduces overall labor and setup costs, and minimizes disruptions to your schedule.

Yes, Ramos Roofing Plus is fully licensed (AR License # 039289, OK License # 800052) and carries general liability and workers' compensation insurance. Our crews are trained to install exterior systems according to manufacturer guidelines and building codes.

We offer dual warranty protection: a manufacturer's material warranty (premium siding and gutters offer long warranties) and our workmanship warranty. Our workmanship warranty protects you against installation errors, ensuring a reliable installation.

Yes, coordinating a siding replacement and a roof replacement is highly efficient. Replacing both systems at the same time ensures a complete weather-tight seal for your home's exterior envelope and saves you money on consolidated labor and staging costs.

We recommend washing vinyl siding once a year with a garden hose and soft brush to remove dirt. Gutters should be cleared of leaves in spring and fall, unless gutter guards are installed. Regular inspections prevent minor issues from turning into major repairs.
