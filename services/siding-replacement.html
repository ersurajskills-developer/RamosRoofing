Exterior Upgrades

Restore and Modernize Your Home's Envelope

If your current siding is cracking, warping, showing signs of wood rot, or fading due to solar UV rays, it's time for a replacement. Faded or damaged siding lets moisture penetrate your walls, threatening the structural framing of your home.

At Ramos Roofing Plus, our siding replacement process begins with the complete removal of old exterior layers. We inspect the underlying OSB sheathing for mold or wood rot, replacing damaged panels before wrapping the house in a Tyvek weather barrier.

We then install your chosen siding material—whether premium vinyl, fiber cement, or engineered wood—providing a fresh, modern aesthetic that significantly increases your home's resale value and energy efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear signs include warping, buckling, or cracking in multiple sections; soft spots indicating wood rot underneath; peeling interior paint or wallpaper pointing to wall leaks; high heating and cooling bills due to compromised insulation; and frequent need for painting due to moisture retention.

Most standard residential siding replacements are completed in 3 to 5 workdays. The timeline depends on the size of your home and the complexity of the architecture. Our crews manage the entire tear-off, sheathing repair, house wrap installation, siding application, trim work, and site cleanup.

Yes, upgrading from vinyl or wood to James Hardie fiber cement is highly popular during replacements. Hardie siding offers a beautiful, premium aesthetic that increases curb appeal and home value. It resists rot, fire, pests, and impact from hail, providing lifetime durability for your home.

Yes, during tear-off we inspect the underlying wood sheathing. If we find dry rot, water damage, or mold, we cut out the compromised boards and install fresh sheathing. This ensures your home's structural wall frame is sound and provides a solid base for the new siding.

If your siding replacement is required due to covered storm damage, such as hail fractures or wind blow-offs, it is typically covered by insurance. We assist River Valley and NWA homeowners by compiling photographic proof of damage and detailed estimates to support their insurance claims.

We install a high-performance weather-resistant barrier (house wrap) over the bare sheathing. We seal all seams, window frames, and doors with specialized flashing tape. This system prevents wind-driven rain from penetrating your sheathing while allowing internal humidity to escape, preventing rot.

We offer premium trim systems in matching or contrasting colors to frame windows, doors, corners, and fascia. This includes durable fiber cement trim (HardieTrim) and low-maintenance aluminum trim wraps. A properly detailed trim system defines your home's architecture and seals transition joints.

While not mandatory, we highly recommend replacing soffits and fascia during a siding replacement. This ensures your home's entire exterior envelope is updated and maintenance-free. We install ventilated vinyl or fiber cement soffits to improve attic airflow and wrap fascia boards in aluminum trim.

We offer dual warranty protection: a manufacturer's material warranty and our workmanship warranty. Premium materials like James Hardie offer a 30-year non-prorated limited warranty. Our workmanship warranty protects you against installation errors, ensuring a long-lasting replacement.
