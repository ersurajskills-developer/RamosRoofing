Maintaining Your Weatherproof Envelope

High winds can rip siding panels loose, while hailstones can leave cracks and holes in vinyl siding. Siding leaks are dangerous because they allow water to run down behind the boards, slowly rotting your home's wooden frame.

Our siding repair specialists respond quickly to inspect and secure loose boards, replace cracked sections, and repair rotting wood fascia or soffits. We work to color-match new materials to your existing siding for a clean finish.

We work with vinyl, fiber cement, wood, and composite siding, providing durable repairs that restore your home's structural protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

The most common causes of siding damage are high winds (which can loosen or blow off panels), severe hail (which can crack vinyl or dent metal), landscape accidents (like lawnmower debris), and long-term moisture retention causing rot on wood siding or backing boards.

Yes, we can perform localized repairs on single damaged siding panels. For vinyl, we use a specialized tool to unlock the damaged panel, pull the fasteners, and slide in a matching replacement panel. For fiber cement, we cut out the damaged section and install a fresh plank.

Cracked, loose, or missing siding leaves your home's wood sheathing exposed to wind-driven rain. Moisture seeping behind siding can cause wood rot, support mold growth inside wall cavities, attract pests, and compromise your home's drywall. Prompt repairs prevent these expensive structural damages.

Matching siding color can be challenging due to sun fading. We source panels from leading manufacturers in matching profiles. For painted siding (like wood or fiber cement), we take a physical scrap sample to match the exact faded paint code, ensuring a clean, seamless finish.

Yes, we handle all aspects of exterior trim repair. This includes replacing rotted wood fascia boards, repairing damaged aluminum trim wraps around windows, securing loose vinyl soffit panels, and repairing corner posts. A sealed trim system is essential to prevent moisture and pest entry.

If your siding is damaged by a covered event like severe hail storms or high-wind gusts, repairs are typically covered by insurance. We provide detailed photo reports and estimates for insurance adjusters, helping you document the damage and navigate the claim process.

Warped or melted vinyl siding (often caused by heat reflection from high-efficiency windows on neighboring homes or outdoor grills) cannot be straightened. The damaged panels must be removed and replaced with new panels. We also recommend addressing the heat source to prevent reoccurrence.

Siding repair costs depend on the material (vinyl is highly cost-effective, while wood or fiber cement requires more labor), the size of the damaged area, and accessibility. Minor repairs are very affordable. We provide clear, itemized written estimates before beginning any work.

We remove the damaged siding panels to expose the wall sheathing. We cut out all rotted sheathing boards, inspect the wall framing, repair any damaged studs, install new sheathing, apply a waterproof house wrap barrier, and then install the new siding panels and trim.

Requesting an estimate is simple. Call our office at (479) 652-1424 or fill out our online request form. We will schedule a technician to inspect your siding, diagnose the damage, and provide a clear, competitive estimate for your repairs.
