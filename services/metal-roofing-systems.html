A Lifetime Roof For Home and Business

If you want to buy your home's last roof, a premium metal roofing system is the answer. Ramos Roofing Plus installs standing seam and rib metal panel systems that are built to last 50+ years.

Standing seam metal roofing features vertical panels joined by raised interlocking seams. The fasteners are completely hidden inside the seams, protecting them from weathering and eliminating the leaks associated with exposed screws.

We use heavy-gauge steel finished with heat-reflective Cool Roof coatings. This system reflects solar heat, keeping your attic cooler in the summer and lowering your monthly energy bills.

Frequently Asked Questions

We install high-end standing seam metal systems and exposed-fastener rib panels. Our premium systems use heavy-gauge steel coated with advanced PVDF/Kynar finishes. These finishes offer outstanding UV and fade resistance, ensuring your roof retains its color and protective properties in severe weather conditions.

Standing seam is the gold standard because it uses hidden fasteners. The panels are secured to the deck using clips underneath the vertical seams, which lock together. This design eliminates exposed screws that can back out or leak over time, creating a completely seamless, waterproof system that lasts 50+ years.

We typically use heavy 24-gauge or 26-gauge steel panels for residential and commercial installations. 24-gauge panels are thicker and offer maximum strength, wind load resistance, and oil-canning resistance, making them ideal for high-end residential custom homes and commercial properties.

We use mobile roll-forming machines to roll and cut the steel panels on-site at your property. This allows us to custom-fabricate each panel to the exact measurements of your roof's runs, eliminating joints and seams, minimizing waste, and ensuring a precise, weather-tight fit.

Standing seam metal roofs offer exceptional wind resistance. The panels are interlocked and clipped securely to the roof deck, leaving no exposed edges for wind to catch. These systems are tested to withstand wind speeds up to 140-160 MPH, making them the most wind-resistant option available.

No. Lightning is attracted to the tallest point in an area, not the material. Because metal is non-combustible and a conductor of electricity, if a metal roof is struck, it safely dissipates the charge into the ground, reducing the risk of fire compared to wood shingles or standard composites.

Standing seam metal roofs can be installed on low-slope roofs (down to a 2:12 pitch, or even lower with specialized double-lock seams). For completely flat or zero-slope sections, single-ply TPO white membranes or flat roof coatings are recommended to prevent standing water from seeping through.

We use high-grade steel panels that are coated with a protective zinc-aluminum alloy (Galvalume) before the color paint is applied. This alloy provides exceptional corrosion resistance. All cut edges are treated during installation, and we use compatible rust-free fasteners to ensure long-term durability.

We offer dual warranty protection: a manufacturer's paint finish warranty (often 30 to 40 years against fading and chalking) and our workmanship warranty. Standing seam systems also qualify for extended structural warranties when installed by our certified crews, ensuring your investment is secure.
