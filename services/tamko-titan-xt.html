High-Wind Armor

Locking Out the Winds of the River Valley

Wind storms regularly sweep through the Arkansas River Valley and Northwest Arkansas, lifting shingle tabs and breaking seal strips. TAMKO Titan XT shingles are specifically engineered to fight wind lift.

The secret is the AnchorTite technology—a tough, reinforced polyester strip built directly into the shingle's nail line. This strip prevents nail heads from tearing through the shingle under high wind pressure, anchoring the entire system to the deck.

Our factory-trained crews are certified to install Titan XT systems. Combined with the shingle's rapid-seal technology, Titan XT roofs lock down quickly after installation to resist winds up to 160 MPH.
