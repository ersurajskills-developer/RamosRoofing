Premium Products

Malarkey Bella Vista Class 4

Authorized installer of Malarkey Class 4 impact shingles. Made with polymer-modified asphalt for premium hail resistance.

Elite Hail Defense

Rubberized Shingles Built to Absorb Hail

Traditional asphalt shingles get brittle over time, making them vulnerable to fractures from hail impacts. Malarkey Bella Vista Class 4 shingles solve this issue by using polymer-modified asphalt.

By blending rubberized polymers with raw asphalt, Malarkey creates a shingle with outstanding elasticity. When hailstones hit a Malarkey Class 4 roof, the rubberized structure absorbs the impact force and bounces back, preventing the asphalt mat from fracturing.

Ramos Roofing Plus is an authorized installer of Malarkey products, offering home and business owners premium storm protection alongside potential homeowner's insurance premium discounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Malarkey Bella Vista Class 4 shingles are modified with rubberized polymers (Flexor asphalt technology) that allow them to absorb the impact of hail up to 2 inches in size without fracturing. They are also made with upcycled rubber and plastics, making them more durable and eco-friendly than standard shingles.

Class 4 is the highest impact resistance rating. During testing, steel balls are dropped onto the shingle to simulate hail. If the shingle does not fracture, it receives a Class 4 rating. This protection prevents hail strikes from bruising the asphalt mat, avoiding premature granule loss and leaks.

Yes, because Class 4 shingles reduce the risk of storm damage, many insurance carriers offer substantial premium discounts (often up to 20-30%) to homeowners who install them. We provide the certification documentation required by your insurer to help you secure these discounts.

Yes, Malarkey is a leader in sustainable roofing. Their shingles are manufactured using upcycled rubber from tires and recycled plastics. Each roof using Malarkey shingles diverts rubber tires and plastic bottles from landfills, and incorporates smog-reducing granules that clean the air.

Malarkey shingles feature a reinforced nail zone and high-strength sealants that anchor them to the deck. They are tested to withstand wind speeds up to 130-150 MPH, preventing blow-offs and wind lift during severe storms in Arkansas and Oklahoma.

Yes, Malarkey shingles are equipped with Scotchgard protector granules. These granules contain copper, which naturally inhibits the growth of Gloeocapsa magma (blue-green algae) that causes dark streaks. This keeps your roof clean and looking new for decades.

Yes, Ramos Roofing Plus is an authorized installer of Malarkey products. Our crews are trained and certified to install their shingles according to manufacturer guidelines, which allows us to offer extended systems warranties that cover both materials and labor.
