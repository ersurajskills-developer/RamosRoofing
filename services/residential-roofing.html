Frequently Asked Questions

We install all major residential roofing materials, including architectural asphalt shingles, Class 4 impact-resistant shingles (Malarkey and TAMKO), standing seam metal roofs, exposed-fastener rib panels, and specialty flat-roof coatings for home additions. We will help you select the best material based on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and storm protection needs.

Most residential roof replacements are completed in just 1 to 2 workdays, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Our crews manage the entire process, from property protection and tear-off to decking repairs, shingle installation, and a final magnetic sweep of your yard for nails, ensuring a fast and clean job.

Our standard architectural shingles carry a wind resistance rating of 110 MPH to 130 MPH. For storm-prone areas in Arkansas and Oklahoma, we install premium shingles like the TAMKO Titan XT, which features a reinforced nail zone and carries a Class H wind rating of up to 160 MPH, protecting your home against severe storm damage.

Yes, we install Class 4 impact-resistant shingles. These shingles use polymer-modified asphalt to absorb the impact of hail up to 2 inches in size without fracturing. Installing Class 4 shingles protects your home against spring storms and often qualifies you for substantial discounts on your annual homeowner's insurance premiums.

If your roof requires replacement due to covered storm events like wind gusts or hail strikes, the cost is typically covered by insurance. We help homeowners compile photo documentation of damage and itemized estimates for their claims, and we meet adjusters on-site to ensure all storm damage is accounted for.

We enforce strict property protection protocols. Before tearing off old shingles, we wrap your home's landscaping, gardens, decks, and windows in heavy-duty protective tarps and plywood shields. After the installation is complete, we perform multiple magnetic sweeps of your yard, flower beds, and driveway to capture all nails.

Proper ventilation is critical to prevent heat and moisture buildup in your attic. Without a balanced system (soffit intake and ridge exhaust), hot air bakes your shingles from underneath, shortening their lifespan and voiding warranties. Ventilation also reduces attic temperatures, lowering your summer cooling bills.

Yes, standing seam metal roofing is an excellent residential option that lasts 50+ years. When installed over solid roof decking with high-quality underlayment and attic insulation, metal roofs are no noisier during rain than traditional shingles. They offer a modern, premium look and outstanding energy efficiency.

We offer dual warranty protection. You receive a manufacturer's material warranty (typically a lifetime warranty on premium architectural shingles) and our dedicated workmanship warranty. As certified installers for major brands, we can also offer extended systems warranties covering both materials and labor.

You can request a free estimate by calling our team at (479) 652-1424 or filling out the quote request form on our website. We will coordinate a time for an inspector to visit your home, evaluate your roof's condition, discuss material options, and deliver a detailed, transparent estimate.
