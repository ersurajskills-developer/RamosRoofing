Water Shedding

Engineered for Zero-Slope Performance

Flat roofs require specialized materials and techniques because they do not benefit from gravity-assisted runoff like pitched roofs. At Ramos Roofing Plus, we specialize in high-performance flat roof installations that prevent standing water from penetrating your building envelope.

We install single-ply thermoplastic polyolefin (TPO) membranes, hot-air welded at the seams to form a continuous water-tight seal. For high-traffic commercial decks, we offer modified bitumen multi-ply asphalt systems that stand up to physical wear and punctures.

Additionally, we apply premium elastomeric acrylic and silicone coatings to older flat roofs. This process restores waterproof integrity and reflects UV rays without the expense of a full structural tear-off.

Frequently Asked Questions

We install premium flat roofing systems, including single-ply TPO white membranes, multi-ply modified bitumen asphalt systems, and liquid-applied elastomeric silicone coatings. We help you choose the best system based on your roof's usage, structural slope, building code compliance, and budget.

Flat roofs are engineered with a very slight slope (typically at least 1/4 inch per foot) to direct water toward drains, scuppers, or perimeter gutters. During installation, we use tapered insulation boards to create positive drainage paths, preventing standing water from pooling on your roof deck.

The most common causes of flat roof leaks are deteriorated seams, clogged drains that cause ponding water, cracked flashing around HVAC curbs, and physical punctures. We prevent leaks by heat-welding TPO seams, checking flashings, clearing drains, and performing regular, professional preventive maintenance.

Yes, flat roofs are highly repairable. If a leak is caused by a localized seam failure or puncture, we can patch the area using heat-welded TPO patches or specialized sealants. We trace the leak from the interior stain to the exterior entry point to ensure a permanent, water-tight repair.

Silicone roof coating is an excellent, cost-effective restoration option for older, structurally sound flat roofs. We clean the roof, check seams, and apply a thick, seamless liquid silicone membrane. This coating seals all micro-cracks, resists ponding water, and extends the roof's life by 10 to 15 years.

TPO is much lighter, cleaner, and faster to install than traditional built-up (tar and gravel) roofs. TPO's heat-welded seams form a continuous, seamless waterproof barrier. TPO's white surface also reflects up to 80% of solar heat, making it far more energy-efficient than dark gravel systems.

Flat roofs require more regular maintenance than sloped roofs because they gather debris and leaves. We recommend checking scuppers and drains at least twice a year to prevent ponding water, inspecting seams and flashings, and removing heavy debris to ensure water can drain freely.
