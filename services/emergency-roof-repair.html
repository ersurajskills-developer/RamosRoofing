Immediate Protection When Disaster Strikes

Heavy storms, straight-line winds, and sudden falling trees don't wait for business hours. At Ramos Roofing Plus, our emergency roofing response is active 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our primary objective during an emergency dispatch is "dry-in stabilization." We secure high-visibility, heavy-duty tarps over exposed roof sections, valleys, and structural breaches to prevent rain from destroying your home's interior framing, drywall, and belongings.

Once the immediate water entry is stabilized, we schedule a full damage assessment and document all structural losses with high-resolution photos to support your insurance claim process.
